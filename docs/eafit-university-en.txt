HISTORY
-------
EAFIT was founded in 1960 by a group of entrepreneurs in Medellín who wanted to
train qualified professionals for the region's growing industry.

Key milestones:
- 1960  Founded as "School of Management and Finance" by Medellín entrepreneurs
- 1962  Opened Textile Technology, Industrial Programming, and Mechanics faculties
- 1971  Officially recognized by the Colombian Ministry of Education (Act 759, May 6)
- 1975  Reorganized as a full university; began offering five-year engineering programs
- 1996  Opened first branch outside Medellín (Llanogrande campus)
- 1997  Established School of Social Sciences and Humanities
- 1999  Created Product Design Engineering and Law faculties
- 2002  Launched Mathematics Engineering — first such program in Colombia
- 2003  First institutional high-quality accreditation (6-year period)
- 2004  Added Engineering Physics, Social Communication, Political Science programs;
        renamed campus "Universidad Parque" (University-Park)
- 2008  Received Lápiz de Acero Award for campus public spaces design
- 2010  Accreditation renewed for 8 years (March 2010); Urbam research center founded
- 2011  Added Psychology and Marketing majors
- 2026  Current accreditation valid through 2026; QS expanded to 7 subject areas

ACCREDITATION & RANKINGS
-------------------------
- All undergraduate programs hold "high quality" accreditation from the Colombian
  Ministry of Education.
- First Colombian university to earn two consecutive institutional accreditations.
- Current accreditation valid through 2026.
- QS World University Rankings by Subject 2026: present in 7 subject areas
  (expanded from 3 in previous edition).
- Ranked #1 among Colombian universities for ESG (Environmental, Social, Governance)
  reputation.
- Only Colombian higher education institution in President Obama's
  "100,000 Strong in the Americas" initiative.


RESEARCH & INNOVATION
----------------------
EAFIT's research system is called "Sistema CTel" (Science, Technology and
Innovation System). It covers: Research, Innovation & Technology Transfer,
CTeI Culture, and CTeI Training.

Research and Impact Centers:
  Valor Público  — Public value and knowledge management connected to society
  Urbam          — Urban and Environmental Studies Center (founded 2010, A1-rated
                   research group). Manages Bachelor's in Urban and Habitat Design.
                   Focuses on sustainable urban planning and social projection.
  In-sight       — Leadership Impact Studies and Incidence Center
  On.going       — Entrepreneurship and impact startup center
  Imaginar Futuros — Observatory for futures and foresight studies

Other research infrastructure:
  - Research groups and seedbeds within each school
  - Asia Pacific Studies Center (at La Aguacatala)
  - Confucius Institute branch (partnership)
  - U Ventures fund — supports university entrepreneurship ventures
  - Centro Cultural Biblioteca Luis Echavarría Villegas (Library & cultural center)

Recent highlights (2026):
  - Innmetec startup (EAFIT-CES research) selected as DeepTech Pioneer 2026
  - AREUEA International Conference 2026 hosted at EAFIT — first time in Latin America
  - 100 Research Scholarships awarded to strengthen the Science & Technology District
  - Argos Center for Innovation under construction
